About Us

Empowering Connections: Crafting Digital Experiences That Inspire.

The Digiplus Connect Cafe provide a one-stop solution for various business service needs. Our Connect Cafe offers a range of service including printing, photocopying, scanning, passport photographs, and binding of documents and sales of MTN Enterprise Business Connectivity and IoT Products such as Mifis, Routers, 5G boxes, Wi-Fi cameras, Doorbells, etc.

Our primary objective is to offer convenience, reliability, and exceptional customer service to both individual and corporate clients.
